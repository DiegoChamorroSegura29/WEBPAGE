About Me

My passions

Hi! I’m a first-year Software Engineering student at SDU with a strong interest in building practical and scalable digital solutions. My background combines software development, AI experimentation, and creative work such as video editing and 3D design, giving me a versatile and problem-solving mindset.

Outside of my academic and technical work, I maintain an active and disciplined lifestyle. I have played football for over ten years and founded my own team, experiences that have strengthened my leadership, teamwork, and communication skills. I also practice individual sports such as athletics and gym training, which have helped me develop consistency, resilience, and a strong work ethic.

I am actively involved in volunteering, where I have contributed to organizing running clubs, participated in student organizations, and collaborated in activities related to animal care. These experiences have reinforced my sense of responsibility and ability to work with diverse groups of people.

I also have a strong interest in philosophy, economics, and personal development. I enjoy reflecting on decision-making, human behavior, and long-term thinking, often through writing and podcasts. This interest extends into practice, as I have explored small-scale investments in cryptocurrencies and early-stage companies, developing an early understanding of markets and risk management.

In addition, I have worked as a freelance video editor and collaborated with small businesses, gaining experience in client communication, adaptability, and delivering results in real-world scenarios.
